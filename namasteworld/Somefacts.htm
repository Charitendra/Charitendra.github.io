 Real Facts about Bharat:

 

  • The World's first university was established in Takshila in 700BC... more than 10,500 students from all over the world studied more than 60 subjects.

 

  • The University of Nalanda built in the 4th century BC was one of the greatest achievements of ancient Bharat in the field of education.

 

  • Sanskrit is the mother of all the European languages. Sanskrit is the most suitable language for computer software reported in Forbes magazine, July 1987

 

  • When many cultures were nomadic forest dwellers over 5000 years ago, Bharatians established the Harappan culture in the Sidhu Valley (Indus Valley Civilization).

 

  • Rig Veda is acclaimed as the oldest extant literature available to humans.

 

  • Ancient Bharat claims to have been the origin of Judo and Karate. Something similar to karate was called Kalaripayate.This art from seems to have traveled from Bharat to the countries of the far-east along with the Buddhist religion

 

  • Most Prolific Nonfiction Writer The late Osho Rajneesh is credited with over 650 books translated into 32 languages making a total of 1, 150 published titles.

 

  • Longest Epic The Maharahtra, composed by Veda Vyasa around 1000 B.C chronicles the battle between the children of two brothers, pandu and Dhritarashtra, in more than 1,00,000 slokas.

 

  • Most Languages/Dialects Of the 7,000 languages and dialects spoken in the world, 845 are Bharatian

 

  • Oldest Recorded Language Sanskrit, the Indo-Aryan language is the oldest recorded language of the world, Rig Veda being the first Sanskrit prevalent during 2000-500 B.C.-1000 A.D. and later into modern Sanskrit.

 

  • Oldest Living Language Tamil represents certain literary types not found in Sanskrit or other Aryan languages and traces its history to Tolkappiyam, the earliest extant manuscript of Tamil grammer, dated 500 B.C.
